Artist Statement


Hi! My name is Sam, and I'm a programmer, musician and digital artist from Montreal, Qc. Initially interested in visual arts and music, I've studied classical music at McGill university, and eventually sought to integrate my practices by taking on programming and following the Computation Arts degree at Concordia University.

I've gained experience with all sorts of software and languages, such as html5, javascript, Unity/C#, Max/Msp, Ableton, Cubase, iZotope, Blender, Arduino, Vyv Photon and Mad Mapper. I currently work as both teaching assistant and research assistant at Concordia and seek to pursue my career as generalist programmer and audio specialist in the video game industry.
