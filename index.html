Samuel is a digital artist and musician from Gatineau, QC. Raised around the world, he graduated from a bachelors of music at Mcgill university in 2016 and currently studies Computation Arts at Concordia university.

Samuel is interested in interactive media, game design, as well as digital music and sound technology. He likes participating in game jams and game design competitions such as GMTK jam, Ubisoft Gamelab competition, and js13k, as programmer and/or sound designer. He currently works as teaching assistant and research assistant at Concordia university.
